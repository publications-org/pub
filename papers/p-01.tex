\section{Property}

$\forall S \in \mathbb{R}, \quad x < Baud(MAX)$
\begin{align*}
Freq(S) = \frac{Hi(S)}{Lo(S)} \cdot Weight(x)\\
-\frac{Weight(S)}{Freq(S)} = \frac{Hi(S)}{Lo(S)}
\end{align*}
Thus by Hartley's Law, the weight of $S$ over its frequency may be equal of the division between the highest signal and lowest signal of S.
